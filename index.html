About Misibis Bay

Welcome to Misibis Bay, an exclusive luxury resort nestled on the pristine shores of Cagraray Island in Albay, Philippines. Surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and the iconic Mayon Volcano, we offer an unparalleled escape for those seeking a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and indulgence.
